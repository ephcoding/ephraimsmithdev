slash

Follow the journey of one of Rock's greatest axe men.

  • 1965

    Saul Hudson, later nicknamed "Slash", is born in Hampstead, London to Anthony and Ola Hudson.

  • 1985 - 1986

    Axl Rose and Izzy Stradlin ask Slash to join the newly formed Guns N' Roses with Duff McKagan and Steven Adler. The band would proceed to write some of there most prolific material and end up signing with Geffen Records in 86'.

  • 1987

    All hell breaks lose with the release of APPETITE FOR DESTRUCTION which would go on to become the best-selling debut album of all-time.

  • 1991

    After heading out on a two-and-a-half-year tour for the USE YOUR ILLUSION tour, Guns' release USE YOUR ILLUSION 1 and 2 debuting in the top 2 spots. This has never been accomplished by any other band.

  • 1993

    Slash plays what would be the last show of his first stint with Gn'R.

  • 1994

    The band Snakepit is born and records material written by Slash originally rejected by Guns. They would go on to record two albums, IT'S FIVE O'CLOCK SOMEWHERE, and AIN'T LIFE GRAND.

  • 1996

    In 1996 Slash officially announces his departure from Guns N' Roses.

  • 2002

    Slash disbands the Snakepit and reunites with Duff McKagan and Matt Sorum to form the super-group VELVET REVOLVER fronting the band with STONE TEMPLE PILOTS lead singer, Scott weiland.

  • 2008

    Even after two successful albums, Velvet Revolver fires Weiland due to "chemical issues" and goes on an indefinite hiatus in 2011.
    In September, Slash starts working on his first solo album which would include other big-name artists such as Ozzy Osbourne, Chris Cornell, Adam Levine, and Miles Kennedy of Alter Bridge.

  • 2010

    Self-titled album SLASH, drops in April, debuting at No. 3 on the U.S. charts.

  • 2012

    Slash's second solo album, APOCOLYPTIC LOVE is released. This time he teamed back up with MILES KENNEDY AND THE CONSPIRITORSto record the entire album.

  • 2014

    Slash featuring Miles Kennedy and the Conspirators release their second collaboration and Slash's third solo album, WORLD ON FIRE.

  • 2016

    For the first time in 23 years, Slash plays a "warm-up" gig with Guns N' Roses where it all started, at the Troubadour in LA. Subsequently, they would head out on the aptly-named NOT IN THIS LIFETIME tour.

  • 2018

    Slash continues to tour with the reunited Guns' and releases his fourth solo album with Miles Kennedy and the Conspirators, LIVING THE DREAM.

"You can't wait around for destiny to give you what you think you deserve, you have to earn it, even if you think you've paid your dues." - Slash
